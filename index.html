About Us

Best Burger
in your City

Just over a year ago, we became fascinated by the idea of discovering your hunger through someone else's taste. What if you could eat anything you ever wanted? Or eat whatever you desire in your mind? It may sound crazy and unhealthy, but we wanted to satisfy the closest thing to risk taking. While there are many other places where you can eat, we realized there is no better way to experience a burger right now than through Cluckin' Bell. A burger might be bad for you, but your happiness is all that we care about.
